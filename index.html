SYSTEM COMPROMISED

80% of humanity was wiped out in weeks. The Plague didn't care about borders or bank accounts. But in the middle of the Pacific, one stronghold remains: The Next Petrol Archipelago.

For years, this cluster of islands was the world's battery and pharmacy, managed by a fragile alliance of corporate titans: Rosskrild & Silva, Gethulius, and Exxoil. The islands were safe. Until they got greedy.

A military rescue mission brought back "survivors." One was asymptomatic. Now, the military facility has fallen, the infection is loose, and the corporate paradise has turned into a tropical hell.

YOU ARE NOBODY. A third-party contractor. A fix-it guy. You have three objectives. Choose wisely, because the island is listening.

[ DIE ]

Succumb to the rot, the rogue military factions, or the unforgiving jungle.

[ DOMINATE ]

Harvest wood, scavenge scrap metal from abandoned refineries, and distill your own fuel. Build a fortress in the deep woods to ride out the infection.

[ ESCAPE ]

Navigate the corporate web, complete NPC quests, and signal the continent for a rescue that might never come.

CORE PROTOCOLS

  • PROCEDURAL DECAY: The game is 100% Procedurally Generated. Every time you wake up, the layout of military bunkers, resource drops, and infected densities shift entirely. Adapt or die.
  • UNCOMPROMISING SURVIVAL: Deep, grid-based mechanics honoring the golden age of complex survival simulations. Manage your inventory, craft weapons, and monitor your vitals.
  • DYNAMIC QUEST SYSTEM: NPCs are survivors with their own agendas. Helping a scientist might grant immunity boosters but anger the military remnant. Your choices carry weight.
